Welcome to Rio Cristal

We are an environmental conservation initiative. Our purpose is to involve local inhabitants in preserving nature and develop sustainable alternatives. Explore Ecuador's beauty while participating in conservation efforts!

About the Program

This volunteer program offers participants the opportunity to engage in environmental conservation projects while learning the Spanish language and discovering the beautiful culture and landscapes of Ecuador. Volunteers will live alongside local inhabitants and experience the diversity of Ecuador's natural ecosystems.

The program lasts from one to three months, with a focus on nature, language learning, and tourism. Volunteers work four hours a day and have Spanish classes for one hour. The afternoons and weekends are dedicated to relaxation and exploration.

What We Do

We work with the local community to promote nature conservation and environmental education through various activities:

  • Spanish Classes
  • Biodiversity Inventory
  • Trail Construction & Signage
  • Cabin & Recreational Place Construction
  • Organic Agriculture
  • Environmental Education for Children
  • Production of Artisanal Chocolate & Fruit Wine

Accommodation

Volunteers will stay in a sustainable house built with eco-friendly materials. Each volunteer will be provided with a tent for outdoor excursions and camping trips. Meals will be provided three times a day, with options for vegetarian, vegan, and other dietary restrictions.

A washing machine is available on site, and volunteers may either do their own laundry or request assistance for a small tip.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How long do volunteer programs last?

Programs range from one to three months.

Do I need to be a biologist to participate?

No, anyone passionate about nature, adventure, and learning Spanish is welcome to join.

What is the minimum age to participate?

Participants should be 18 years or older. There is no age limit.

How many hours do volunteers work?

Volunteers work for four hours each day, with the afternoons free.

How many hours of Spanish classes will I have?

You will have one hour of Spanish classes per day, from Monday to Friday.

How much free time will I have?

You will have free time every afternoon after Spanish classes and every Sunday is a free day.

Cost Breakdown

Understanding the costs associated with your volunteer program is essential for planning. Below is a breakdown of the primary expenses:

  • Flights:

    Flights to and from Ecuador are to be arranged and paid for by the volunteers themselves. Please check various airlines for the best deals and plan your travel dates accordingly.

  • Food:

    Meals are provided three times a day at the accommodation. However, if you wish to explore local dining options or have special dietary requirements, you may incur additional costs.

  • Accommodation:

    Accommodation is included in the program fee. You will stay in a sustainable house with eco-friendly materials. Each volunteer is provided with a tent for outdoor excursions and camping trips.

  • Travel Car:

    Transportation for excursions and activities is not included in the program fee. Volunteers are encouraged to budget for local travel costs, including car rentals or public transportation for weekend trips and outings.

If you have any questions about specific costs, feel free to reach out to us!
